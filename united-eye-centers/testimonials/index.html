Patient Testimonials

Many of our patients write us to express their satisfaction with United Eye Centers. These are just an example of the letters we receive each year.

Dear Dr. Conahan,

I just wanted to take the time to say “thanks” for fixing my vision! Since having the LASIK correction of my vision, my life has changed. I imagined what it would be like without glasses but didn’t even realize myself just how freeing this is.

You and your staff were very professional and managed to put me at ease with the procedure, even though I tend to be quite a “wimp” when it comes to anything with my eyes. I felt completely prepared for the procedure and the recovery period, which was pleasingly uneventful. The entire process was surprisingly painless.

My only regret is that I didn’t do this sooner. Thanks again for improving the quality of my life and for being my eye physician.

-- MC, M.D.
