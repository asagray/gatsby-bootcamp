About Dr. Vervaet

Dr. Keri Vervaet received her B.S. in Exercise Physiology from the University of Wyoming in Laramie. She completed her Doctorate of Optometry at Pacific University, College of Optometry in Forest Grove, OR. Dr. Vervaet provides comprehensive eye exams for all ages. She is a member of the American Optometric Association and the Colorado Optometric Association.

Before moving to Colorado in July, Dr. Vervaet and her family lived in San Antonio, TX where she served as an optometrist in the US Air Force. While in the Air Force, she provided eye exams for active duty military members, their families and retirees. She also ran the Air Crew Contact Lens program that provided contact lenses for pilots and aircrew members

Dr. Vervaet now lives in Littleton with her husband and three childreen. In her free time, she enjoys running, hiking and spending time with family and friends.
